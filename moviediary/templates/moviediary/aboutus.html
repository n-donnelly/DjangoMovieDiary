About Us

This is a small website created by myself, Neil Donnelly that allows users to place movies on their wishlist and review them for other users to see. It is not much more than a prototype and demo for possible employers, I do not aim to make any money form this or even advertise it. It is simply a pet project that I hope you enjoy and can make use of.
I named this Roger Mebert in tribute to the great film critic Roger Ebert. This is why I use the 4-star rating system rather than the conventional 5-star system I hope with this site, for anyone who finds it can use it as a means of recording their own thoughts and opinions on movies they've seen regardless of what anyone else thinks.
